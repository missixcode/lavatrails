Hawaii Volcanoes National Park awaits your arrival with beautiful hiking trails, sweeping landscape views, and unlimited outdoor adventure. Schedule a hiking trip with our tour guides to experience the adventure of a life time!

THE KĪLAUEA VOLCANO

The Hawaii Volcanoes National Park is home to two volcanoes: Kīlauea and Manuna Kea. Kīlauea is the most famous of the Hawaiian Volcanoes, and rightfully so! As the youngest and most active volcano, you can sometimes see its lava flowing into the ocean or glowing the the Halema'uma'u crater that crowns the beautiful park grounds.

THURSTON LAVA TUBE

The Thurston lava tube is a unique lava tube close to the Kīlauea Iki parking lot. A beautiful, 20 minute (1/3 mile) walk through a tree fern forest will take you to the illuminated cave-like lava tube. This attraction is open to visitors 24/7, but is only illuminated during the daytime. Schedule a hike today to experience this once-in-a-lifetime sight!
